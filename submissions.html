Modern Migration

If you would like to submit work to us, please follow the guidelines below:

  1. General submissions should be no longer than 1000 to 2000 words, except for opinion pieces, which can run to a maximum of 3000.
  2. Please let us know in advance if you would like to submit a long-form piece of up to 10,000 words.
  3. Include at least one image with your submission.
  4. We are happy for contributors to republish work to their own blogs. If you wish to write on the same topic for another publication, you are welcome to but please use a different angle.
  5. Modern Migration is a platform to publish news stories, features and opinion pieces related to migration, immigration and refugees. It is not a platform for hate speech or any kind of dicrimination.
  6. We strive to keep your work your own and will inform you of any changes made prior to publication.
  7. All submitted work must be original. Please provide credit for quotes and images where necessary. Privacy of confidential sources will always be respected.

Unfortunately, we cannot provide payment for your work at this time. However, we are working towards growing the site to a point where we can reimburse writers for their time and hard work so please stick with us until then.
