Modern Migration

Modern Migration is a non-profit collective of writers, researchers and photographers.

We publish news, opinion and long-form investigative journalism related to all aspects of migration in the modern world, including immigration and the refugee crisis.

Our long-term goal is to explain and perpetuate understanding of how modern migration works and the ways in which it affects our everyday lives.

Please visit our submissions page for guidelines on how to submit your work.
